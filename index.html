WHAT IS OUR BUSINESS?

We noticed that some schools have no canteen, and it is sometimes a hassle for our parents to provide us with food. Teachers also complain about the food system of the school! In fact, a number of school which have canteens complain about the food hygiene and structure there. So, to solve this unsolved dilemma, our team has set-up to open a chain of canteens in the school. So, we bring to you "ESSEN" to solve all your food problems!


We found many root causes and intent to solve them. They are The dietary needs of a child at school are not met at times. Instead of healthy and cleaner foods, they have breads, noodles and other detrimental foods. Parents who are under the pressure of work cannot cook for their childwhich results in the child needing to have unconventional food likes, sandwiches, burgers etc. The lucrative condition of a parent might not as good to buy healthier and cleaner versions of food. The sodium and fat intake in young children is heigher than the protein intake.


According to a survey that we took, 9 out of 10 people going to school would prefer our canteen over normal school canteen, which increases the chances of our profit. The value we add to a child's food habits accomodate why we are good for our customers. The parents are ensured that their child is eating clean and good food. Moreover they even get insights of what is going into their child's stomach. It also reduces the parents' worries on making food everyday.


Also according to, our literature survey the CAGR of Indian Food Service Market is 11.19%, and we are sure that we would surpass this rate with higher annual and monthly growth rates.
